Delete Your WeOut Account & Data

Developer: Bogdan Pupeza
App Name: WeOut

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the WeOut app and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Account.
  3. Select Delete Account and follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Alternatively, you can email your deletion request to pupezabogdan01@gmail.com using the email address associated with your account.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

  • Deleted Immediately: Your profile information, email address, location data, and usage statistics are permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Retained Temporarily: Some technical logs and backup data may be retained for up to 30 days for legal and security purposes, after which they are automatically deleted.
  • Data Not Deleted: Any information you have shared publicly (such as event comments) may remain visible to other users unless you delete it before account removal.

Need Help?

If you have questions or need assistance with deleting your account, please contact us at pupezabogdan01@gmail.com.

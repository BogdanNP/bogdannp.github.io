Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2024

TL;DR: Fair Playr is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information is stored locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Fair Playr is designed with privacy in mind. The app operates entirely offline and does not collect any personal information from its users.

Local Data Storage

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Player Information: Names and skill levels of players you create
  • Match History: Records of matches including teams, scores, and dates
  • Skill Evaluations: Player skill adjustments made after matches

This data is stored using Hive, a local NoSQL database, and remains exclusively on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

All data stored by Fair Playr is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Managing your player roster
  • Generating balanced teams based on player skills
  • Tracking match history and results
  • Evaluating and adjusting player skill levels
  • Providing statistics and insights about your matches

Your data is never transmitted to external servers, third parties, or any online services.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Fair Playr does not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks have access to your information
  • No cloud services are utilized for data storage or backup

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since Fair Playr stores all data locally on your device:

  • Data security is managed by your device's operating system
  • We recommend using device-level security features such as screen locks and encryption
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception during network communication

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and backing up your data if desired.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it:

  • You can delete individual players or matches within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data from your device
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings will delete all information

There is no server-side data storage, so once deleted from your device, the data is permanently removed.

6. Third-Party Services

Fair Playr does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions completely independently without external dependencies.

7. Children's Privacy

Fair Playr does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the app operates entirely offline with local storage, it complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

If we make material changes to how we handle user data (such as introducing online features), we will notify users through an app update notice.

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Modification: You can edit player information and match details through the app interface
  • Deletion: You can delete individual items or all data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Your data remains on your device and is not locked into any proprietary format
